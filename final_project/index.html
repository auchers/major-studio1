Agricultural Crop Profiles of Countries in Sub-Saharan Africa

Each vertical bar below represents one country in Sub-Saharan Africa.

Countries are represented by a visual profile of their top 10 crops. Each texture represents one crop type and its height corresponds to the proportion that crop holds of the country's top ten (according to yield).

By default, the countries are sorted from left to right by worker productivity (as measured by the agricultural value added by each worker in 2010 US dollars).
