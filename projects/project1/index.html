Does Inequality Lead to Revolution?

Gini Coefficient, A Measure of Inequality

The gini coefficient is a measure of how equally a nation's wealth is dispersed. These can range from 0 - 1 with a 0 expressing perfect equality and 1 expressing total inequality (100% of a nation's wealth belonging to 1 person).
